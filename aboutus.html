Our Mission & Vision

In Uganda, there is roughly only 1 psychiatrist for every 1 million citizens. Mind Bridge was created to fill the gap between struggling alone and accessing professional care.

The Problem

High costs, geographic isolation in rural areas, and the stigma that views mental illness as "witchcraft" prevent many from seeking help.

Our Solution

A web-based platform designed specifically for Sub-Saharan African users, offering anonymous support and culturally relevant resources.

.

Our Impact

We aim to reduce the time it takes for a person to access support and provide data for future policy advocacy.
